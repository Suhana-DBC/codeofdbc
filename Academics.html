  ACADEMICS    

  • Eligibilty

    • A pass in Higher Secondary or any other Examination accepted by the University as equivalent thereto with any science subject combination with a minimum of 45% marks in aggregate.Those who passed Vocational Higher Secondary course will get an additional weightage of 25 marks in the ranking index.

  • Duration of the course

    • The duration of B.Voc. Software Development course shall be three years consisting of six semesters. The duration of each semester shall be five months inclusive of the days of examinations. There shall be at least 90 working days in a semester and a minimum 540 hours of instruction in a semester.

  • Programme Structure

    • The B.Voc Software Development shall include:

  • Language courses (English)
  • Course Structure
  • General Education Components
  • Skill Components
  • Project
  • Industrial Training
  • Soft Skills and Personality Development Programmes
  • Study tours

  • Course Structure
